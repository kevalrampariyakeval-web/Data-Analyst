# 📖 Project Story

Businesses generate large amounts of sales data every day, including customer purchases, product performance, revenue, and returns. Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to identify growth opportunities, monitor sales trends, and improve business performance.

To solve this challenge, I developed an interactive **Sales Performance Dashboard** using **Power BI**. The dashboard combines executive-level KPIs, customer insights, and product analysis into a single interactive report, helping businesses track performance and make data-driven decisions.

The objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful visual insights that support smarter and faster business decisions.

# 🎯 Project Objectives

This dashboard was created to answer important business questions such as:

✅ What is the total sales revenue?

✅ How many orders and customers does the business have?

✅ Which products generate the highest sales?

✅ Which customers contribute the most revenue?

✅ What is the return rate?

✅ How are monthly sales performing?

✅ Which product categories perform best?

✅ Which products require business attention?
